What do we require?
We are looking for members who genuinely want to improve and help the guild, therefore we try to make our requirements almost non-existent.
  • A minimum of 7.5k average actions per day is applied, anything over this amount is adequate.
  • A minimum contribution of 1.05b in value of required materials per week is required, this is equivalent to 150m per day.
  • A 5% resource tax is applied, though occasionally it may be risen on lowered temporarily.
  • You must be nice to fellow members, we attempt to maintain a positive attitude in all circumstances.
  • We require that whenever a personal event occurs that will make a your average actions fall, that you inform an officer.
  • If at any point you fall below these requirements an officer will notify you, though we encourage you to handle it independently.
  • We require you to be awesome, inspire awe everywhere you go, as well as in everyone you meet.
